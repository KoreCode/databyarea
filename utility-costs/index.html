Before You Move In

  • Ask for recent bills: request 12 months of electricity, gas, water, sewer, and trash where available.
  • Check heating fuel: natural gas, electric heat, propane, oil, and heat pumps produce different winter budgets.
  • Confirm fees: ask whether water, sewer, trash, recycling, stormwater, or HOA utilities are separate.
  • Verify internet: check actual address availability, equipment fees, upload speed, contract terms, and promotional expiration.
