How to Lower Utility Bills

  • Ask for recent bills: For rentals and home purchases, request the last 12 months when available.
  • Separate rate from usage: A low rate can still mean a high bill in a large or inefficient home.
  • Check heating fuel: Natural gas, electric resistance, heat pump, propane, and shared-building heat each budget differently.
  • Re-shop internet: Promotional rates often expire after the first year, so budget the regular price.
