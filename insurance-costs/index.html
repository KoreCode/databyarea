Quote Checklist

  • Match coverage: compare dwelling, liability, personal property, collision, comprehensive, and medical coverage consistently.
  • Match deductibles: cheaper premiums can hide larger wind, hail, roof, collision, or comprehensive deductibles.
  • Ask about exclusions: roof age, sewer backup, flood, hail, replacement cost, and actual cash value terms can change value.
  • Compare the package: bundle discounts can help, but separate carriers may still produce a better total cost.
