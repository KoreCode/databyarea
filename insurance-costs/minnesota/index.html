Monthly Insurance Planning Ranges

These ranges are not quotes. They help estimate the insurance portion of a Minnesota household budget.

Homeowners $110-$310

Single-family home planning range, heavily affected by replacement cost, roof age, deductible, and weather risk.

Auto $90-$240

One vehicle with typical coverage. Younger drivers, violations, full coverage, or high-value vehicles can raise this.

Renters $12-$35

Common low-cost coverage for personal property and liability, with higher limits or riders increasing premium.

Health Varies

Plan choice, employer contribution, subsidy eligibility, network, and household size dominate the final monthly cost.

Quote Checklist

  • Match coverage limits: Compare dwelling, liability, personal property, collision, comprehensive, and medical coverage consistently.
  • Check deductibles: A cheaper premium may hide a much larger wind/hail, roof, or auto deductible.
  • Ask about exclusions: Roof age, water backup, sewer backup, flood, hail, and replacement-cost terms can change the value of a policy.
  • Bundle carefully: Home and auto bundle discounts can help, but compare the total package against separate carriers.
