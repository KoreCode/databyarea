About Data By Area

We make U.S. location-based cost and rate data easier to explore. Our pages are designed to be quick, clear, and useful for everyday comparisons.

What this site is

Data By Area is a public data directory focused on geographic comparisons across the United States. We organize information into simple state-by-state pages so visitors can scan patterns quickly.

How to use Data By Area

Start with a category, choose a state, and compare values with nearby or national benchmarks. For major decisions, always combine this information with local quotes and official sources.
