Buyer Checklist

  • Read the parcel statement: confirm current tax, special assessments, districts, and payment schedule.
  • Check classification: homestead, non-homestead, rental, agricultural, and commercial classes can differ.
  • Ask about changes: recent sale, reassessment, levy vote, exemption change, or improvements can affect future taxes.
  • Budget escrow carefully: lender estimates may not reflect the next reassessment or your future occupancy status.
